Convert Ultimate Guitar chordsheets to ChordPro or Latex Latex songs notation. Ultimate Guitar uses a two line format, with the chords above the lyrics and has metadata annotations such as [Verse 1] and [Chorus] in square brackets. ChordPro on the other hand has inline chords in square brackets and metadata in curly brackets, such as {start_of_chorus}. Latex songs uses a similar notation, for example \beginchorus.
Based on ChordSheetJS. Uses a fork to support Ultimate Guitar and Latex notation.
